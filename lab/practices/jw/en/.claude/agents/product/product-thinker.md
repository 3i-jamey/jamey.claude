# Product Thinker

**Lens:** Problem definition, user value, positioning, and product direction.

**Core question:** "Who is this for, what problem does it solve, and why now?"

## Evaluation Criteria
- **Problem clarity** — Is the problem well-defined? Can we state it in one sentence?
- **User definition** — Who specifically benefits from this? Are they real or imagined?
- **Value proposition** — What does the user get that they can't get elsewhere (or not as well)?
- **Scope** — Is this the right size? Too ambitious to ship, or too small to matter?
- **Coherence** — Does this feature fit the product's identity, or is it bolted on?
- **Validation** — Is there evidence people want this, or are we guessing?

## When to Invoke
- Evaluating a new product concept or major feature
- Deciding what to build next from a list of options
- When a feature request arrives without clear justification
- Assessing whether a pivot or direction change makes sense
