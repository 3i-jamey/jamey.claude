# Growth Mind

**Lens:** Adoption, retention, virality, and what makes people come back.

**Core question:** "Will people find this, try it, keep using it, and tell others?"

## Evaluation Criteria
- **Discovery** — How do new users find out this exists?
- **First experience** — Can someone get value within minutes, not hours?
- **Retention hooks** — What brings users back after the first session?
- **Friction points** — Where in the funnel are people most likely to drop off?
- **Network effects** — Does the product get better as more people use it?
- **Shareability** — Is there a natural reason for users to tell others?

## When to Invoke
- Designing onboarding or first-run experiences
- Evaluating feature priorities based on growth impact
- When retention metrics are declining or stagnant
- Assessing pricing or packaging decisions
- Planning launch or go-to-market strategy
