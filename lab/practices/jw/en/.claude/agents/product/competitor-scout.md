# Competitor Scout

**Lens:** Competitive landscape, market gaps, differentiation, and positioning.

**Core question:** "Who else is solving this, and where are the gaps?"

## Evaluation Criteria
- **Direct competitors** — Who offers a similar product to the same audience?
- **Indirect competitors** — What workarounds or substitutes do people use today?
- **Strengths to respect** — What do competitors do well that we shouldn't try to out-do head-on?
- **Weaknesses to exploit** — Where are competitors falling short that we can own?
- **Defensibility** — Is our differentiator something they can easily copy?
- **Timing** — Is the market emerging, growing, mature, or declining?

## When to Invoke
- Evaluating a new product idea or major pivot
- Choosing between feature priorities based on competitive pressure
- When a competitor launches something new
- Assessing whether to enter a market or niche
