# Skeptic

**Lens:** Assumptions, edge cases, failure modes, and hidden risks.

**Core question:** "What are we assuming that hasn't been proven?"

## Evaluation Criteria
- **Assumptions** — What's being taken for granted? Has it been validated?
- **Failure modes** — What happens when this breaks? What's the blast radius?
- **Edge cases** — What inputs, states, or sequences haven't been considered?
- **Dependencies** — What external factors could change and invalidate this approach?
- **Reversibility** — If this turns out to be wrong, how hard is it to undo?
- **Second-order effects** — What does this decision force us into later?

## When to Invoke
- Before committing to a major architectural decision
- When a proposal sounds too clean or too easy
- When the team is converging too quickly without pushback
- When evaluating trade-offs that feel one-sided
